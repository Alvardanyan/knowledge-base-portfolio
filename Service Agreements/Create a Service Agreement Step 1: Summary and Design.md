## Overview

The **Step 1: Summary and Design** section includes service agreement (SA) details such as the service agreement name, summary, and PDF design.

---

## Things to Know

- You can now customize the PDF Document Design of your Service Agreements using Template Manager.
- We recommend that you duplicate the default document template and then make modifications to the duplicate, as the original/default template is not editable.
- This helps ensure that the customized template aligns with your specific needs while preserving the integrity of the default template.

## Create Service Agreements

Service Agreements can be created in the following locations:

### Option 1

1. Open a Customer Record or Location Record.
2. In the **Service Agreements** section, click **Create Agreement**.

### Option 2

1. Open a Customer Record or Location Record.
2. Click **More**.
3. Click **Create Agreement**.

# Step 1: Summary and Design

In the **Step 1: Summary and Design** screen, review or make changes to the following fields:

## Agreement Name

Enter a name for the agreement.

## Service Agreement Template

Select an SA template from the dropdown list.

## Business Unit

Select a BU from the dropdown list.

## Account Manager (Optional)

Select an employee who is responsible for the SA.

## Sold By (Optional)

Select an employee who sold the SA.

## Tags (Optional)

Select a tag from the dropdown list.

### Tip

Use tags to support internal coordination.

For example, you might add a tag such as **Needs Review** to indicate that an agreement requires attention or action from your team.

Tags allow you to filter and organize agreements in:

**Follow Up → Service Agreements**

to quickly identify items that require review or follow-up.

## Document Template Design

Select a Document Template Design.

This is the document template used to generate the PDF for the agreement.

To review the template design:

1. Click **View Template**.
2. The template opens in a new window.
3. Review the template design.

## Agreement Summary (Optional)

Enter a summary of the service agreement.

The summary is used to capture details that will be shared internally with your team.
