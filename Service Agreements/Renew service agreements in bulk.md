# Renew service agreements in bulk

## Overview

Renew multiple expiring service agreements at once to save time and reduce repetitive manual work.

## Who uses this feature

- Administrators and CSRs
- Primarily benefits Commercial Service and Replacement business types
- Applies to all trades

## Feature configuration

- Account configuration is required to use this feature. Please contact Technical Support for details.

## Renew service agreements in bulk

1. Go to the navigation bar and click Follow Up > Service Agreements.
2. In the side menu, click Service Agreements.
3. From the Service Agreements page, select the agreements that need renewal.
4. Click Actions, and from the dropdown select Renew Agreements.
5. From the pop-up that opens, click Renew Agreements.

The Agreements Renewed Successfully message appears to confirm that the agreements have been renewed.

## View errors

View, troubleshoot, and re-run the bulk actions if you received an error message.

1. Click View Errors on the error message.

On the drawer that opens, you can view the details of the error.

> **Note:** You can only renew service agreements in the Activated status. This creates renewals for each agreement in the Draft status.

1. Click the Agreement ID to open the agreement in a new tab and fix the errors.
2. When finished, return to the drawer and click Re-run Renew Agreements.

## Understand possible errors

View and understand the error types when renewing the agreements in bulk.

| Error Type | Error Details |
|------------|---------------|
| Invalid Data | Business Unit is deactivated. |
| Invalid Data | Customer is deactivated. |
| Invalid Data | Service Agreement Template is deactivated. |
| Already Renewed | A renewal has already been created. |
| Other | An unexpected error occurred. |
