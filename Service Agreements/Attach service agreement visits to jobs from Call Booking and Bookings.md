# Attach service agreement visits to jobs from Call Booking and Bookings

## Overview
Attach Service Agreement (SA) visits to jobs during the call booking process from the Calls and Bookings screens. This allows CSRs to discover bookable SA visits during a call and attach a single visit to the job, ensuring accurate records and preventing duplicate jobs.

## Who uses this feature
* Administrators, accountants, estimators, project managers, and operations managers.
* Primarily benefits Commercial Service and Replacement, and Commercial Construction business types
* Applies to all trades

## Things to know
* Currently, you can only attach one visit per job. This attaches the unscheduled service agreement visit to the job.
* If there are unscheduled service agreement visits available for a customer's location, a banner with service agreement visits displays on the Call Booking and Bookings screen.

## Attach a visit from Job Booking screen
To attach a service agreement visit to a job from the Job Booking screen:

1. On a call with a customer on the Call Booking screen, click Attach Visit.
2. On the Select Service Agreement Visit pop-up that opens, select a visit you want to attach to a job and click Attach to Job.

The visit appears on a Call Booking screen and fills out the job fields.

> **Note:** Click Detach Visit to remove the visit from a job.

3. Check the job details and click Book job. The service agreement visit gets attached to the job.

## Attach a visit from Bookings screen
To attach a service agreement visit to a job from the Bookings screen:

1. Open the booking request on the Bookings screen.
2. Click Attach Visit.
3. On the Select Service Agreement Visit pop-up that opens, select a visit you want to attach to a job and click Attach to Job.

The visit appears on the Bookings screen and fills out the job fields.

4. Check the job details and click Book job.
