# Targets Overview in Convex

## Overview

Targets serves as your centralized work center for prospecting, allowing you to organize and
manage prospecting lists across properties, contacts, companies, and signals, streamlining them
into a single, simplified workflow.

## Who uses this feature

* Managers, regional managers, representatives, and salespeople
* Primarily for Commercial Service and Commercial Construction business types

## Overview of Targets

Go to the Navigation Panel > Targets. The Targets screen opens, and it has the following
sections:

### Quick Filters

Shows the targets in an easy-access order. It includes these filters:

- My Targets: All targets that are assigned to you.

- My New Targets: All targets assigned to you in the last 5 days.

- Me In Progress: All targets assigned to you that have an open status.

- My Closed Won: All targets assigned to you that have been qualified.

- My Closed Lost: All targets assigned to you that have been disqualified.

- Recently Updated: All targets that have been updated in the last 5 days.

- All: All targets in your organization.

- Non-List Targets: Targets that are not part of a List.

> **Tip:** Click More to collapse the view.

### Lists

Shows all the target lists in your organization. Here you can:

- Use Search to look for the list.

- Click Sort to organize lists by Name or Last Activity.

- Click +Add List to create a new List.

### Target List

Shows the selected target List. It includes the following columns:

- Target Name: Click the name to open the Target drawer that shows details about the
  target, such as Companies, Properties, and Activity Logs.

- Assignee: Employee who is assigned to the target. Click the field to assign an employee
  from the list.

- Last Activity: Most recent interaction associated with a target.

- Leads: Contact associated with the target.

> **Note:** Leads appear after Populating Leads.

- Properties: Property associated with the target.

- Company Sites: Company site associated with the target.

## Perform actions

Manage your table of targets by performing these actions. After selecting the targets, the
Actions menu appears. Here you can:

- Update Assignee: Select an employee you want to assign to the target. When finished, click
  Apply.

- Update Status: Select a status for the target from the list. When finished, click Apply.

- Move to List: Select another list or create a new one to move the selected targets.

- Remove: Removes the target from the list.

- Select All: Selects all targets in a list.

- Clear: Clears selected targets.
