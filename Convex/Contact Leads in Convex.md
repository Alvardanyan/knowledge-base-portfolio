# Contact Leads in Convex

## Overview

After creating your target list, you're ready to begin outreach by contacting those leads directly. This lets you engage potential customers, share your offerings, and move them further along in the sales process.

## Who uses this feature

* Managers, regional managers, representatives, and salespeople
* Primarily for Commercial Service and Commercial Construction business types

## Target drawer overview

Manage targets by updating their status and viewing key information.

Open the target list and click the target you want to view.

The target drawer includes these sections:

* Overview        

Click Assignee to select an employee from the list. The employee is responsible for the contact.

Click Status to select a status for the lead.

* Leads        

Click Populate Leads to generate additional contacts for a target.

Click the contact card to open the contact details panel, which includes Contact Info, Other Info, and Activity. 

* Companies (if applicable)

Click the company card to open the company details panel, which includes Company Site, Contacts at Site, Company Wide Information, and Property.

* Properties (if applicable)        

Click the property card to open the property details panel. For more, see Deep dive into the property details panel.

* Activity Log        

Shows all the activities done with the target.

## Contact Leads

After the contacts are populated for the targets, you can start contacting the leads.

1. Open the target list and click the target you want to contact.

2. From the **Leads** section in the target drawer, select the contact, then:

   - **Click Call**. From the **New Call** pop-up that opens, click **Call** to call the contact.

     > **Note:** Ensure microphone permissions are enabled to call directly from your account.

   - **Click Email**. From the **New Message** pop-up that opens, create the message and click **Send**.

     > **Note:** Ensure your email is connected with Convex to send emails directly from your account.

   - **Click Send to Phone** to send the contact to yourself. From the **Send to Phone** pop-up that opens, enter your phone number and click **Send**.

3. To remove the contact from the target, click the **three dots** on the contact card and select **Remove Contact**.
