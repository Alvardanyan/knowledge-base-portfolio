# Adjust the view with Tilt Map

## Overview

The Tilt Map feature in Atlas allows users to adjust the viewing angle of the map for a dynamic, three-dimensional perspective. This feature provides a better sense of the terrain and layout, enhancing the visualization of buildings and surrounding areas.

## Who uses this feature

* Managers, regional managers, representatives, and salespeople
* Primarily for Commercial Service and Commercial Construction business types

## Rotate the location view with Tilt Map

By tilting the map, you can gain insights that are not apparent from a standard rooftop view, improving your ability to analyze and explore property locations effectively. The tilt feature provides a 360° view of buildings, allowing you to see the front, back, sides, and surrounding properties, not just the roof or top-down perspective.

1. In the navigation panel, click Map. The Map screen opens.

2. From the property list panel, search or select a property.

3. In the property details panel that opens, click Locate on map to view it.

4. On the left-hand side of the map, click the Tilt menu icon.

5. The map view changes, and two new icons appear to rotate the view clockwise and counterclockwise. Click these icons to rotate the map as needed.

> **Note:** You can enter the address you want in a search bar and the Tilt map will keep the view.

6. Zoom in on the map to get a closer view of the property.

> **Note:** To return to the standard rooftop view, click the Tilt Map icon.
